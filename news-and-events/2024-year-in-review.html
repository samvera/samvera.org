The Year in Review and a big thank you

Thank you to everyone in the Samvera Community for your incredible commitment to building and sustaining our work throughout the year. Your activities -- sharing your knowledge in interest groups, leading community initiatives, participating in community sprints, and attending community events - are what keeps this community so vibrant and welcoming. I am truly excited and grateful to help facilitate it all.

Here is a "Year in Review" infographic (PDF) sharing some of what we accomplished this year. Please feel free to share this with anyone else who should know that your participation is valuable and gets results. You can also watch the State of Samvera overview and end of year updates from Samvera technologies on YouTube. Wishing you a joyful holiday season, a well-earned break, and a new year filled with even more collaboration and mutual support!

Heather Greer Klein, Community Manager
